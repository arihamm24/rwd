1, 2, Freddy's Coming for You!

In 1984, director Wes Craven introduced the world to everyone's favorite nightmare: Freddy Krueger. Krueger (popularized by Robert Englund's portrayal shown above) is the antagonist of the Nightmare on Elm Street film series. He came to being after the parents of our "Elm Street Protagonists" burned him alive as punishment for his string of local child murders. Deadly, witty, and hungry for vengeance, Freddy Krueger was a central element of the 80s slasher movement, helping normalize horror and its popularity among teens for decades.
